SPA

Our hotel is perfect for those looking for a tropical vacation within a results-proven spa retreat. A great place to find restoration, seek adventure, and receive all the tools you need to reset your body, mind, and spirit

POOL

Our freshwater family-friendly pool offers a shaded swim up bar at its centre, setting the perfect tone for relaxing and socialising. Our saltwater infinity pool is an irresistible adult-only escape right above the sea. With four Jacuzzis, picturesque views and a stretch of sand, it makes for a truly memorable experience by the water.

GYM

We offer a variety of ways to stay active, healthy and rejuvenated in our hotel, from fitness classes on the beach, to modern equipments in our gym, to award-winning hammam experiences

MARINA

Our marina provides 24-hour self-service fuel Station with ice, restrooms and convenience items and access to parking facilities. Security is offered by monitored surveillance cameras and roaming security staff. Access to one of the Island's most exclusive docking facilities without arduous membership requirements and membership fees

GOLF COURSE

Our resort offers some of the best golf in the world, with three golf courses, The Old Nine, The Country Club and the world-renowned Green Monkey: a Tom Fazio creation carved from an old stone quarry which is exclusive to Sandy Lane guests. Wherever you play, you will be enchanted by the spectacular natural beauty of the terrain and the stunning views of the coastline

RESTAURANTS

Wherever you choose to dine, you will enjoy the perfect match of exceptional cuisine within exquisite surroundings. Our four restaurants celebrate wonderful flavours from around the world, each meal a delight. Convivial bars are styled for a joyful dining experience spent with friends and family.
